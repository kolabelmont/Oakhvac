Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I change my HVAC system's filters?

It's generally recommended to change your HVAC filters every 1-3 months, depending on factors like filter type, household allergies, and pets.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance?

It's advisable to have your HVAC system professionally serviced at least once a year to ensure it operates efficiently and to catch any potential issues before they become major problems.

Why is there ice forming on my air conditioner unit?

Ice formation on an AC unit usually indicates a problem with airflow or refrigerant levels. It's best to turn off the unit and call an HVAC professional to diagnose and fix the issue.

How do I improve the air quality in my home?

You can enhance indoor air quality by using high-quality air filters, ensuring proper ventilation, reducing indoor pollutants, and considering air purifiers or humidifiers based on your specific needs.

Signs my HVAC system needs repair or replacement?

Some common signs include inconsistent temperatures, strange noises, unusually high energy bills, frequent breakdowns, and systems older than 10-15 years.

How can I make my HVAC system more energy-efficient?

Simple steps like regular maintenance, using a programmable thermostat, sealing ducts, improving insulation, and upgrading to energy-efficient models can significantly enhance the energy efficiency of your HVAC system.
